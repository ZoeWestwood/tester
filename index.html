About

Hi! I'm Zoe, I am 23 years old and a web designer living in Fleet, Hampshire. I have always had an interest in techology and IT whilst being in school but it wasn't until collage where I found I had a passion for designing and making websites. From there I went to study Web Design and Development at Southampton Solent University achieving a 2:1 with honours.

I am currently working as a Junior Web Designer at CV-Library within this role I carry out regular website updates, ensuring code is up to date and following our inhouse design system. I also design and code products including emails, bespoke company profiles and job adverts for our clients.

Within my spare time I volunteer as a Guide Leader for 3rd Crookham Guides. I enjoy keeping fit by going to the gym, binge watching Netflix, Disney+ and Amazon Prime. I also throughly enjoy cross stitching and in the future I would like to start desiging my own patterns to sell on Etsy.
